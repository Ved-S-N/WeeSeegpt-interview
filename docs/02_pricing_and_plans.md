# WeSee — Pricing and Plans

WeSee has three plans, billed monthly per workspace:

- **Starter** — 3,000 INR/month. Up to 3 seats, 500 generations/month, 1 brand
  voice profile, community support.
- **Growth** — 9,000 INR/month. Up to 10 seats, 5,000 generations/month, 5 brand
  voice profiles, email support, API access.
- **Scale** — 25,000 INR/month. Unlimited seats, 30,000 generations/month, 20
  brand voice profiles, priority support, SSO.

Annual billing gives a 15% discount. Overage generations on any plan are billed at
2 INR per generation. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle; upgrades
take effect immediately with a prorated charge.
