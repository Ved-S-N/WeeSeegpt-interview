# WeSee — Support SLA

Support response targets by plan:

- **Starter**: community forum only, no guaranteed response time.
- **Growth**: email support, first response within 24 business hours.
- **Scale**: priority support, first response within 4 business hours, plus a
  named customer success contact.

Business hours are Monday to Friday, 10:00 to 19:00 IST, excluding Indian public
holidays. Severity-1 incidents (product fully unavailable) on Scale plans are
handled 24/7 and target a 1-hour first response. Support is reachable at
support@weseegpt.com.
