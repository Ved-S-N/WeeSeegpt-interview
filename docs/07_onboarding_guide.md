# WeSee — Onboarding Guide

To get started: create a workspace, invite teammates from Settings → Members, and
build your first brand voice profile by pasting 3 to 5 samples of your existing
content. The profile is ready in about two minutes.

Next, create a brief describing your goal, audience, and key message. Generate a
draft, edit it in the Studio, then send it to the Review Queue. An approver moves
it to Approved, after which it can be published manually or via the Publish API.

New workspaces on paid plans get a free 30-minute onboarding call. Book it from the
banner on your dashboard during the first 14 days.
