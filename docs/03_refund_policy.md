# WeSee — Refund and Cancellation Policy

WeSee offers a 14-day money-back guarantee on the first paid month of any new
subscription. Refund requests after 14 days are not granted for monthly plans.

Annual plans may be cancelled within 30 days of purchase for a prorated refund of
the unused months, minus a 10% administrative fee. After 30 days, annual plans are
non-refundable but remain active until the end of the term.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 to 10 business days.
Overage charges already incurred are non-refundable. To request a refund, contact
billing@weseegpt.com from the workspace owner's email address.
